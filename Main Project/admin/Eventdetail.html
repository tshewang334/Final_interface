Ministry of Finances

Goods and Services Tax (GST)

  • GST is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based indirect tax levied on most goods and services.
  • Follows a simple design with a uniform tax rate on value addition.
  • All taxes a business pays while providing goods/services are eligible for input tax credit.
  • Businesses can claim credit against tax collected when filing returns with DRC.
